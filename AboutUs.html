About Us

Our Mission

From the moment you walk through our doors, we want you bombarded with an atmospheric futsal nirvana that hints at the euphoria to come. The sights, the sounds, the pro-level amenities – it all seamlessly ushers you into a state of rapt anticipation. And then finally stepping onto our flawless indoor pitch—it’s like being born again as a futsal disciple.

That’s our mission: make every second at Team Ground an utterly transcendent journey into the soul of this beautiful game so you don’t just understand futsal but feel it radiating from the deepest core of your being. It’s about living, breathing, and experiencing the passion in its purest form.

Our Vision

Our vision stretches far beyond just these slab walls. We want to be the radiant spark that ignites a futsal renaissance across this entire country, show the world that Bangladesh is a hotbed of futsal glory challenging the game’s European meccas and produce waves of homegrown talent blazing new trails on the global circuit.

In short, we strive to create the ultimate futsal revolution, starting right here at Team Ground Sports Arena, elevating this art, this passion, this culture to unprecedented heights and giving the local talent the world-class home they deserve to let their greatness shine.

Why Choose Us

Provide Quality Futsal Field

Sure, you could go play at one of those run-down, slapped-together indoor fields. But why would you want to when you can experience the crème de la crème of futsal facilities? We’re not just throwing down some turf and calling it a day. Nope, our fields are precision-engineered masterpieces designed to bring out the beautiful intricacies of the game.

Standard International

And we're not just tooting our own horns here - our fields meet the highest international standards for futsal.

A Place for all

It is designed with an intention to bring people together, no matter what their origin, age, background, beliefs or economic status is. It is a place for all people to have fun, maintain good health and connect with each other as a part of an united society.

Play, enjoy, and live 90 minutes of ultimate futsal with your team. Experience the thrill of the game on our high-quality turf, designed for a top-notch playing experience. Make your time here safe and relaxing with the amenities we offer, including a locker room, shower room, and drinking-water supply.
